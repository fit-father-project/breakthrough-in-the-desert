01
The Honest Part

Why v1 reads as a template.

The current page is not bad. It is average, in the precise sense that matters: every fork took the safe default. A brand is a set of choices. An average is the absence of them. Three research streams confirmed the same six tells.

×

Fraunces + Inter

The single most AI-generated serif and sans pairing of 2024 to 2026. Together they signal that no typographic decision was made.

×

Cream, terracotta, gold

The "warm trustworthy wellness" palette. Pleasant, softened, averaged, and forgettable. It has no tension and no owned note.

×

Centered everything

Centered text is the lowest-risk composition. It removes the entry point and the eye-path. It reads as the absence of a layout system.

×

One global fade-up

Every block rises 20 pixels on the same easing. Motion as a default setting, not as choreography with intent.

×

Midjourney hero imagery

Over-rendered light, dreamy haze, plastic skin. For a real event with real people, this is the loudest tell of all. It whispers that the event may not exist.

×

The ✦ sparkle mark

The most overused "tasteful AI" glyph of the era. It is iconography that belongs to everyone, which means it belongs to no one.

02
The Essence

What the brand is.

The desert has always been where people go to break open. The brand is not a wellness event. It is a threshold — and the page should feel like standing at one.
Threshold Elemental Reverent Earned Still

Every brand in the men's-work and sacred-space field splits into two camps. One manufactures depth with symbols, gradients, and hype: wolves, Spartan helmets, glowing chakras, "unleash your warrior." The other earns it through restraint — few marks, real photographs, deep space, and plain language about real human experience.

Breakthrough belongs unambiguously in the second camp. The single biggest separator the research found is this: credible brands look like they were made by people who were actually there. Cheap ones look assembled from spiritual clip art. Every decision in this system serves that one principle.

03
Typography

A voice, not a default.

Two serifs and one grotesk. A characterful display serif carries emotion, a literary text serif carries the reading, and a quiet grotesk handles labels in tracked capitals — the carved-in-stone, ceremonial register.

Newsreader Display · Headlines Soulful and editorial, with a genuinely beautiful italic. Distinctive where Fraunces is generic. Set it light, large, and tightly tracked.

Color

The mineral desert.

Not cozy cream. The benchmark is Amangiri: monastic, sun-bleached, elemental. A tight tonal range of bone, stone, and shadow — warm, low-saturation, with one note of fire and one cool counter-tone for tension.

Ember is the only saturated color in the system. It appears rarely — on the primary action, one word in a headline, the mark. Scarcity is what gives it heat. The old page spent rust and gold everywhere; this one rations them.

The page alternates bone-light and ink-dark sections like the desert moves through a day. Dark sections carry the weight: the founder, the threshold, the close. Light sections carry the air.

The Signature Device

The Threshold.

One mark, owned by this event alone. A doorway: two posts, an arch, a centerline where you cross. It retires the sparkle and becomes the brand's shorthand — section marker, divider, bullet, favicon.

The Threshold Mark

Section marker

Opens each major section in place of a generic eyebrow rule.

List bullet

Replaces the ✦ sparkle on every highlight and feature list.

Contour lines

A secondary device: topographic desert contours as faint section dividers and texture, never as loud pattern.

06
Imagery & Art Direction

Real, or nothing.

The fastest way to kill the AI smell is evidently real photography. Documentary, available-light, grainy, golden-hour. The landscape is a character. Faces show vulnerability, not flexed jaws.

Shoot & source this
  • Real 2025 attendees and speakers, mid-emotion, candid
  • Pre-dawn and golden-hour desert; long raked shadows
  • Wide frames: a lone figure small against vast rock and sky
  • Firelight, the circle, stone, sand, water, smoke
  • Black-and-white reportage moments for gravity
  • Texture close-ups: plaster, stone grain, skin, sand
  • One consistent warm grade; visible film grain
Never this
  • AI-generated or Midjourney hero imagery
  • Stock smiling groups and staged hands-on-shoulders shots
  • Saturated postcard sunsets and lens flares
  • Wolves, lions, Spartan helmets, screaming men
  • Glowing chakras, dreamcatchers, "tribal" patterns
  • Clinical blue color grading; perfect plastic skin
  • More than one image fighting for a single viewport

The current AI hero is the first thing to replace. Until real desert photography is commissioned, lead with the strongest real 2025 photograph.
