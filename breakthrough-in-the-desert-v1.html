I built Breakthrough in the Desert for those people. For the ones who feel the call to experience their full potential but haven't found the doorway. For the ones who know — somewhere deep — that they have more in them than they've been letting out.

The Three-Day Arc

Three days. One journey. A different you.

Most events are a stack of unrelated talks. Breakthrough in the Desert is a curated journey. Each day moves you one step further across the threshold — from waking up to where you are, to coming alive in your body, to stepping into who you're meant to be.

Here's the arc of our journey.

FRIDAY · AWARENESS
Wake up to where you are. See the patterns. Name what's been holding you back.

"What if you could see — clearly, for the first time in years — exactly what's been quietly draining your life?"

That's the question Dr. Anthony will put in front of you within the first hour of the weekend. You'll arrive at the Arizona Grand Resort, find your seat among 150 aligned humans, and step into the experience that will rewire how you experience the next chapter of your life.

Dr. Anthony will open the day with his keynote on Intentional Living — a framework for looking honestly at the dimensions of your life, the energy leaks quietly draining your vitality, and the patterns you've been running on autopilot for years. You'll reflect, share at your table, and start sketching a real plan for where you want to go.

Throughout the day, you'll move between the main stage and experiential movement breakouts — stretching, exercise, breathwork, hands-on workshops, and vendor hall conversations. The afternoon will feature a live Q&A health panel on what it actually takes to build healthspan after 40. The day closes with a special evening keynote, followed by an alcohol-free happy hour featuring Kava — where the conversations get deep and real.

Wake up the body. Reconnect with what's alive in you. Step into vitality.

The desert wakes up early. By 8am you're on a mat, breathing with 149 other people, the sun rising over South Mountain, your body opening for what's about to happen next.

Saturday is the day your body, mind, and spirit come fully online.

You'll start with a 60-minute yoga and movement session led by John Salisbury — one of Phoenix's most respected master yoga teachers. From there, Dr. Jamil Sayegh takes the main stage with his Wake Up keynote — a presence-driven, soul-level invitation to stop sleepwalking through your one life. The morning continues with a somatic breathwork experience designed to reconnect you with the deeper signals and sensations your body has been trying to send you for years.

The afternoon brings two of the most clinically powerful sessions of the weekend: Dr. Amie Hornaman presents on thyroid, metabolism, and the hidden hormonal drivers of midlife fatigue and weight gain — followed by breakout sessions including Dr. Philip Ovadia on heart health and the metabolic truths your cardiologist never told you.

Saturday evening is the moment many attendees remember most. Maraya Brown will lead an honest, soul-level keynote on relationships, intimacy, and reclaiming the spark that's gone missing in long-term relationships and marriages — followed by a sunset pool party at the resort, where the community starts to feel like family.

Release what no longer serves you. Commit to your next chapter. Walk out changed.
