A Letter From the Founder

I built this for the
version of me I want to be at sixty.

I'm Dr. Anthony Balduzzi. Over the past decade our team has helped over 70,000 people across 100+ countries reclaim their health. I'm grateful every day for the work we do.

But I'm going to be honest with you. A few years ago, a question started rising in me — and in my wife Brooke — that we couldn't shake: Is this all we're here to do?

I'd noticed that the people getting the deepest results in our programs weren't just losing weight. They were waking up. Repairing marriages. Reconnecting with their grown kids. Finding deep purpose. Coming alive in ways that had nothing to do with the scale.

So I did my own work. I sat in ceremony. I learned to play the Native American flute. I studied with men's mentors. I spent time in the desert and in the darkness. I let myself be cracked open by the parts of life a medical degree never prepared me for.

Health is the doorway. Aliveness is the room.

I built Breakthrough in the Desert for the people who feel the call but haven't found the doorway. The ones who know — somewhere deep — that they have more in them than they've been letting out.

I'd love to have you with us in the room.

— Dr. Anthony Balduzzi

The Full Agenda

Three days. One journey. A different you.

Most events are a stack of unrelated talks. Breakthrough is a curated journey. Each day moves you one step further across the threshold — from waking up to where you are, to coming alive in your body, to stepping into who you're meant to be.

Here's the arc.

Friday · Awareness
Wake up to where you are. See the patterns. Name what's been holding you back.
"What if you could see — clearly, for the first time in years — exactly what's been quietly draining your life?"

Dr. Anthony will open the day with his keynote on Intentional Living — a framework for looking honestly at the dimensions of your life, the energy leaks quietly draining your vitality, and the patterns you've been running on autopilot for years.

Throughout the day, you'll move between the main stage and experiential movement breakouts — stretching, exercise, breathwork, hands-on workshops, and vendor hall conversations. The day closes with a special evening keynote, followed by an alcohol-free Kava happy hour — where the conversations get deep and real.

Friday Highlights
  • Welcome & Opening Keynote with Dr. Anthony: Intentional Living
  • Experiential breakouts: workouts, breathwork, stretching, somatic practices
  • Afternoon Q&A health panel featuring Caroline Alan and special guests
  • Curated vendor hall featuring leading-edge health and wellness brands
  • Connection lunch (clean food — no seed oils, no compromises)
  • Special evening keynote — speaker to be announced
  • Alcohol-free Kava happy hour to close the day
Saturday · Activation
Wake up the body. Reconnect with what's alive in you. Step into vitality.

The desert wakes up early. By 8am you're on a mat, breathing with 149 other people, the sun rising over South Mountain, your body opening for what's about to happen next.

You'll start with a 60-minute yoga and movement session led by John Salisbury. From there, Dr. Jamil Sayegh takes the main stage with his Wake Up keynote — a presence-driven, soul-level invitation to stop sleepwalking through your one life.

The afternoon brings two clinically powerful sessions: Dr. Amie Hornaman on thyroid, metabolism, and hidden hormonal drivers — followed by Dr. Philip Ovadia on heart health and the metabolic truths your cardiologist never told you.

Saturday evening is the moment many attendees remember most. Maraya Brown leads a soul-level keynote on intimacy and reclaiming the spark in long-term relationships — followed by a sunset pool party at the resort.

Saturday Highlights
  • Sunrise yoga & movement with John Salisbury
  • Wake Up keynote with Dr. Jamil Sayegh
  • Somatic breathwork & body reconnection session
  • Dr. Amie Hornaman: Thyroid, Hormones & the Hidden Drivers of Midlife Fatigue
  • Dr. Philip Ovadia: Stay Off the Operating Table — Heart Health Myths Debunked
  • Maraya Brown evening keynote: Reclaiming Aliveness & the Spark
  • Sunset pool party at Arizona Grand Resort
Sunday · Integration
Release what no longer serves you. Commit to your next chapter. Walk out changed.

Something shifts on Sunday. People who arrived as strangers Friday are now eating breakfast together, sharing what cracked open the night before. The room feels different. You feel different.

The day opens with optional cold plunge and morning movement. Then comes a heart-opening cacao ceremony led by Luana Marzluff — a contemplative, sensory experience that creates the conditions for the integration work that follows.

Dr. Jamil returns mid-morning for a deep workshop on releasing the emotional patterns, limiting beliefs, and inherited stories that have quietly run your life. Dr. Jamil's session has the potential to transform how you live for the rest of your life.

The weekend closes with Dr. Anthony on the main stage for a powerful final session — designed to help you commit to your next chapter and walk out the door already living it.

  • Optional sunrise cold plunge & morning movement

  • Afternoon breakout sessions

  • Closing keynote with Dr. Anthony: Walking Through the Doorway
The Venue

Where breakthrough happens.

Arizona Grand Resort · Phoenix, Arizona

The desert has been a place of transformation for as long as humans have existed. Jesus. Moses. The Buddha. Indigenous vision quests. Sufi mystics. There's a reason the great traditions all sent their seekers into the desert when something inside them needed to break open.

You'll spend three days in that same landscape — not roughing it, but rooted in it. The Arizona Grand Resort sits against South Mountain, the largest municipal park in the country, with miles of sunrise hiking trails just steps from the door. The venue is 10 minutes from Phoenix Sky Harbor and a short drive from Sedona and the red rock country if you want to extend your trip.

This is your wellness vacation, your retreat, and your breakthrough — all in one place.
