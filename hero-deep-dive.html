04 · The play

What I'd actually ship.

Go with a real photograph.

The AI hero will always have at least a 5% tell at scrutiny. A real photo is free, legally clean, and unmistakably real. The cost of switching is zero. The Diehl violet-dusk is my recommendation; the Bee wide-vista is the warm-mood alternative if "sacred initiation" feels too somber.
