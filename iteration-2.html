The promise of healthy
was always too small.

You came up the mountain of healthy. The labs are good. The body is functional. And still — something is missing. Healthy was the floor. Aliveness is the room. We came to the desert to find the door.
What we fixed
Original Direction A had body copy in Bone on a warm Bone-tinted dark gradient — readable for younger eyes, mush for 40+. Why this is the answer
Solves the readability problem inside the same emotional shape you already approved. Dark moody pivot section keeps the dawn-to-dusk rhythm of the page. The Ember radial in the upper right adds atmosphere without touching the text contrast.
