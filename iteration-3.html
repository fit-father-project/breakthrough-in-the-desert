Money

Is this worth the price?

Why this much for three days?
The price reflects what we build: 8 faculty, the Arizona Grand Resort, small attendee count, and a year of post-event support. It's a fraction of the average comparable wellness retreat once you factor in faculty and continuity.
Are payment plans available?
Yes. 3-month and 6-month plans at checkout. No interest, no fees. The 3-month plan is the most common.
What's included vs. extra?
Included: all programming, meals, integration sessions, and the year-long follow-up. Extra: travel and hotel. We have a negotiated rate at the resort.
The Body

What if I'm not in great shape right now?

Do I need to be fit to come?
No. The protocol is built for the body you have, not the one you wish you had. Most attendees are not "fit." This is the room where that's allowed.
What if I have an injury or condition?
The intake form covers this. We modify movement, food, and integration for what you need. No one gets pushed beyond what's safe.
The Spirit

Will this conflict with what I already practice?

Is this religious or spiritual?
Neither, in the formal sense. We don't import a tradition. We make a room where whatever is true for you can come to the surface.
Will my spouse fit in if they don't usually do this stuff?
Most of our attendees bring a partner who'd never do this on their own. They leave saying it was the version of these things they could actually say yes to.
