Privacy Policy

Last updated · May 20, 2026

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information you provide when you visit this event registration page for Breakthrough in the Desert 2026, hosted by The Fit Father Project and The Fit Mother Project.

What we collect

  • Registration information: name, email, mailing address, phone number, ticket selection, partner add-on details, dietary or accessibility needs you provide.
  • Payment information: handled by our checkout provider (Spiffy). We do not store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Site analytics: anonymous aggregate data (pages viewed, device type, referrer) via standard analytics tools.
  • Cookies: functional cookies for session management. No third-party advertising cookies.

How we use it

  • To process your event registration and send event-related communications.
  • To coordinate accommodations, dietary restrictions, and accessibility needs with the venue.
  • To send relevant follow-up communications about future Fit Father / Fit Mother events. You can unsubscribe anytime.
  • To improve the registration experience.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • We do not share your information with sponsors or vendors without your explicit consent.
  • We do not retain payment card data on our servers.

Photo and video

Our team will capture photo and video during the live event for future marketing and community storytelling. By attending, you grant permission for your image to be used in promotional materials. If you'd prefer to opt out, notify our team at registration and we'll honor your preference.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing support@fitfatherproject.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

This site uses minimal functional cookies for session continuity and may use analytics cookies (Google Analytics) to understand aggregate traffic patterns. By using this site you consent to this use. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Contact

Email support@fitfatherproject.com or text 602-999-0004 with any privacy questions.
